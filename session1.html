Harry potter

The Harry Potter movies are a beloved fantasy film series based on J.K. Rowling’s bestselling novels. The series consists of eight films released between 2001 and 2011, produced by Warner Bros. They follow the journey of Harry Potter, a young boy who discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns magic, makes lifelong friends, and faces dark forces. The central storyline focuses on Harry’s battle against Lord Voldemort, the most feared dark wizard, who killed Harry’s parents and seeks to conquer the wizarding and non-magical worlds. Alongside his best friends, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, Harry grows from an innocent child into a courageous young man prepared to risk everything to protect others.

  2. Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets (2002)
  3. Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban (2004)
