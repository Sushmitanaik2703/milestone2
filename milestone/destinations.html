Destinations Around Kudla

Panambur Beach

A vibrant beach known for water sports and stunning sunsets.

Someshwara Beach

Famous for its huge rock formations (Rudra Shile) and scenic views.

Kadri Manjunatha Temple

Historic temple on Kadri Hill dedicated to Lord Manjunatha. Scenic and spiritual.

Tannirbhavi Beach

A calm, scenic beach with tree-lined surroundings and a peaceful vibe.

Pilikula Nisargadhama

An integrated nature park: zoo, lake, science centre, and a heritage village.

Kudroli Gokarnatheshwara Temple

A significant temple in Kudla built by Narayana Guru, known for its architecture.

Polali Rajarajeshwari Temple

A temple dedicated to Goddess Rajarajeshwari, known for tradition and festivals.

Tagore Park

A peaceful park with green lawns and a small lighthouse, perfect for relaxation.
